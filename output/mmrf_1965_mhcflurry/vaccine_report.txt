  6. MANUFACTURING NOTES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • In-vitro transcription (IVT) from linearized DNA template
  • Use CleanCap AG for co-transcriptional 5' capping (Cap1)
  • Substitute UTP with N1-methylpseudouridine-5'-triphosphate
  • Purify by cellulose chromatography to remove dsRNA contaminants
  • Formulate in lipid nanoparticles (LNP) for delivery
  • Recommended LNP composition: ionizable lipid/DSPC/cholesterol/PEG-lipid
  • Target dose range: 25-100 μg mRNA per injection
  • Storage: -20°C (LNP formulated), -80°C (bulk mRNA)
